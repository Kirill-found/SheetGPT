# Privacy Policy for SheetGPT AI Assistant

**Last Updated: December 3, 2025**

## Introduction

SheetGPT AI Assistant ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Google Sheets Data**: When you use the Extension, it accesses the data in your currently active Google Sheets spreadsheet to process your queries. This data is sent to our servers solely for the purpose of generating AI-powered responses.
- **User Queries**: The text queries you submit through the Extension interface.

### Automatically Collected Data
- **License Key**: If you have a premium subscription, your license key is stored locally and sent with requests for authentication.
- **Usage Statistics**: Anonymous usage counts for subscription management.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Process your natural language queries and generate spreadsheet formulas, analysis, and insights
- Provide AI-powered assistance for your Google Sheets tasks
- Manage subscription limits and authenticate premium users
- Improve our service quality

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Your license key and preferences are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
- **Server Processing**: Spreadsheet data sent to our servers is processed in real-time and **not permanently stored**. Data is used only for the duration of processing your request.
- **Encryption**: All data transmission between the Extension and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your spreadsheet data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Store your spreadsheet data permanently on our servers

We may use third-party AI services (OpenAI) to process queries. Data sent to these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Stop using the Extension at any time
- Request information about data we process
- Request deletion of any stored data

## Google API Services

This Extension uses Google API Services. Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
